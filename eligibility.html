Check Donation Eligibility

To donate blood, please ensure you meet the following criteria:

  • You are between 18 and 65 years of age
  • You weigh at least 50kg (110 lbs)
  • You are in good general health on the day of donation
  • No fever, cold, or flu symptoms in the last 2 weeks
  • Minimum 3 months gap since your last donation
  • Hemoglobin level is at least 12.5 g/dL
  • Not pregnant or recently delivered (within 6 months)
